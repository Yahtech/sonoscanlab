About Sonoscan Lab

Founded in 2013, Sonoscan Medical Diagnostic Center has been contributing it's fair quota to excellent medical diagnostic services in the Federal Capital Territory and it's environs. What started as a small diagnostic center has grown over the years to now providing diagnostic services to thousands of patients annually Our journey began with a simple mission: to make accurate and timely diagnostic services accessible to everyone. By this mission we strive to accomplish a vision we hold dear in our hearts. And this vision is to offer services aimed at contributing to the prevention of non-communicable diseases ( NCDs) in Nigeria and reducing the mortality rate thereof. Over the years we have gradually invested in the requisite equipment and assembled a team of skilled and committed professionals to deliver on this vision and mission. We welcome you to Sonoscan.

Diagnostic Laboratory and Imaging Services

PLEASE NOTE: You can recieve your test results by either PDF to WhatsApp, By Email or In person and If you have any ENQUIRIES concerning any other tests, please chat us up on our WhatsApp portal to see which tests are available in your area.

HAEMATOLOGY

Our HAEMATOLOGY lab offers an array of tests ranging from Full blood count, Genotype, blood group to blood clotting screening such as PTT/INR

CHEMICAL PATHOLOGY

Our CHEMICAL PATHOLOGY lab cover tests such as various types of blood sugar checks including OGTT, HBA1c. Others include liver function test, kidney Function tests, Lipid profile and a host of other chemistry tests.

HORMONAL ASSAYS

HORMONAL ASSAYS including female reproductive hormonal tests such as AMH, Estrogen, progesterone, prolactin etc etc. Other hormones for both males and females are also available.

MOLECULAR BIOLOGY

MOLECULAR BIOLOGY Tests such as the various types of viral LOADS including Hepatitis B, hepatitis C, HIV etc

SEROLOGICAL TESTS

SEROLOGICAL TESTS such as HIV, Syphillis, Hepatitis B and C Screening, Hepatitis B profile. Tuberculosis screening and rheumatoid factor

MICROBIOLOGY

Our MICROBIOLOGY Lab covers all the various types of culture tests such as fungal culture, blood culture and urine, stool, sputum, and all forms of swab cultures.
