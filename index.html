ABOUT SONOSCAN DIAGNOSTIC LAB

Founded in 2013, Sonoscan Medical Diagnostic Center has been contributing it's fair quota to excellent medical diagnostic services in the Federal Capital Territory and it's environs. What started as a small diagnostic center has grown over the years to now providing diagnostic services to thousands of patients annually Our journey began with a simple mission: to make accurate and timely diagnostic services accessible to everyone. By this mission we strive to accomplish a vision we hold dear in our hearts. And this vision is to offer services aimed at contributing to the prevention of non-communicable diseases ( NCDs) in Nigeria and reducing the mortality rate thereof. Over the years we have gradually invested in the requisite equipment and assembled a team of skilled and committed professionals to deliver on this vision and mission. We welcome you to Sonoscan.
