Diagnostics Services

At Sonoscan Lab, we provide comprehensive diagnostic services using standard technology to ensure accurate and timely results. Our diagnostics department is equipped with excellent imaging and testing equipment operated by highly skilled professionals.

Our Diagnostic Services Include:

  • Medical imaging(Ultrasound, ECG, X-ray)
  • Laboratory Testing and Analysis
  • Pathology Services
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Pulmonary Function Tests

Ultrasound Scanning

Our ultrasound department utilizes advanced imaging technology to provide detailed examinations of internal organs, blood vessels, and tissues. We offer a wide range of ultrasound services performed by certified sonographers.

Ultrasound Services:

  • Abdominal Ultrasound
  • Obstetric Ultrasound (Pregnancy)
  • Pelvic Ultrasound
  • Doppler Studies
  • Musculoskeletal Ultrasound
  • Thyroid and Neck Ultrasound
  • Prostate scan (TRUSS)
  • Heart scan (Echocardiography)
  • Vascular ultrasound
  • Follicular Tracking (TVS)
  • Eye scan (Occular B scan)
  • Breast scan

Cardiovascular Services

Our cardiovascular services are tailored towards providing diagnostic studies for heart conditions such as hypertensive heart disease, irregular heart beats, valvular heart problems, heart attacks and blocked blood vessels in the upper and lower limbs (peripheral vascular diseases). These cardiovascular services are listed below.

Cardiovascular Services:

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Echocardiography
  • Vascular Duplex ultrasound scan
  • Stress Testing (Availability Awaited)
  • Holter Monitoring (Availability Awaited)
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation
  • Preventive Cardiology
