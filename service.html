Diagnostics Services

At Sonoscan Lab, we provide comprehensive diagnostic services using state-of-the-art technology to ensure accurate and timely results. Our diagnostics department is equipped with the latest imaging and testing equipment operated by highly skilled professionals.

  • Advanced Imaging (MRI, CT Scan, X-Ray)

  • Endoscopy and Colonoscopy

Ultrasound Scanning

Ultrasound Services:

  • Obstetric Ultrasound (Pregnancy)
  • Pelvic Ultrasound
  • Doppler Studies
  • Musculoskeletal Ultrasound
  • Thyroid and Neck Ultrasound

Laboratory Tests
